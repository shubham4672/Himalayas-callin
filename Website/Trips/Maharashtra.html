Ajanta And Ellora Caves

It is one of the largest rock-cut Hindu temple cave complexes in the world, featuring Buddhist and Jain monuments with
artwork dating from the period 600–1000 Cave 16 features the largest single monolithic rock excavation in the world, the Kailash temple, chariot-shaped monument dedicated to Lord Shiva

Mahabaleshwar

It is a small town and a municipal council in Satara district in the Indian state of Maharashtra. It is a place of pilgrimage for Hindus because the Krishna river has its origins here.
Panchgani

Panchgani, called Paachgani , is a hill station and municipal council in Satara district in Maharashtra, India. Panchgani attracts many tourists throughout the year
